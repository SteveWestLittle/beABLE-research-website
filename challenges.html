Challenges in AI for Disability Support

Critical ethical considerations in technological development

Technology Inaccessibility

Many AI systems are not designed with diverse user needs in mind, creating barriers for persons with disabilities.

  • Inadequate natural language processing
  • Limited gesture recognition
  • Inflexible user interfaces

AI Bias

Training data and algorithmic design can perpetuate systemic biases against persons with disabilities.

  • Underrepresentation in training datasets
  • Discriminatory algorithmic decisions
  • Stereotypical language generation

Data Privacy

Persons with disabilities are particularly vulnerable to data exploitation and privacy breaches.

  • Sensitive personal health information
  • Unique identifiable characteristics
  • Potential misuse of personal data
