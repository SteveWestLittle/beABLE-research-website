Recommendations for Inclusive AI

Strategies to develop more equitable and accessible technologies

User-Centered Design

Involve persons with disabilities throughout the design and development process.

  • Interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Iterative feedback mechanisms
  • Diverse representation in design teams

Ethical AI Development

Implement principles that prioritize inclusivity and reduce algorithmic bias.

  • Balanced training datasets
  • Transparent AI decision-making
  • Regular bias audits

Policy and Governance

Develop governmental and organizational frameworks to support inclusive technology.

  • Accessibility standards
  • Data protection regulations
  • Mandatory inclusive design guidelines
